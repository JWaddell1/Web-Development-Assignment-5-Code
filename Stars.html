The Talents We Produced

Welcome to our Player Showcase, where we highlight some of the most exceptional talents who have helped shape our club’s success. Each athlete featured here has grown through hard work, dedication, and passion for the game—earning achievements both on and off the field. Explore their journeys and celebrate the milestones that make them an unforgettable part of our club’s history.

1.Jason Raskash

Jason Raskash joined the club as a promising 16-year-old academy player in 2016. Known for his pace and technical control, he quickly earned his place in the first team by the following season. Over the years, Jason developed into one of the club’s most reliable attacking midfielders, contributing goals and assists during key tournaments. His consistent performance attracted attention from larger teams, and in 2022 he made a successful move to a European-based club. Jason is remembered for his discipline, leadership, and strong connection with fans.

His acomplishments

  • 1× Defensive Player of the Year
  • 2× Regional Shield Winner
  • 1× Team Leadership Award
  • Helped achieve the club’s lowest goals-conceded season in 2021

2.Samson Abod

JSamson Abod joined the club in 2018 as a defensive powerhouse with exceptional strength and tactical awareness. Starting as a rotation player, he quickly progressed to a full-time starter after demonstrating outstanding consistency and match awareness. Samson became a cornerstone of the club’s defense, helping guide the team to major victories and improving the defensive record each season. His dedication and leadership qualities eventually earned him a transfer to a major regional club in 2023, marking a big step forward in his career.

His acomplishments

  • 2× League Cup Champion
  • 1× Youth Development Award
  • 1× Player of the Season (2021)
  • 15 career goals and 22 assists for the club

3.Jamal Jacobs

Jamal Jacobs joined the academy in 2017 and quickly became known for his creativity, ball-handling, and natural flair. Regarded as one of the most exciting young prospects, he was promoted to the senior team earlier than expected. Jamal progressed rapidly, becoming a key playmaker and fan favorite. His breakthrough season came in 2021, earning him recognition throughout the league. Jamal later left the club in 2024 to join a top-tier international team. He is best remembered for winning the prestigious Google Ball Trophy, awarded for outstanding creativity and technical excellence.

His acomplishments

  • 1× Google Ball Trophy (2021)
  • 1× Young Player of the Year
  • 20 assists in his final two seasons
  • Recognized as club’s top playmaker for two consecutive seasons
