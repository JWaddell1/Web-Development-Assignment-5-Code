Meet our staff

At Jack’s Football Camp, our success is built on the expertise and passion of our coaching and support staff. Each member of our team brings years of experience, dedication, and a genuine commitment to developing every athlete who walks onto our field. From strategy and skill-building to health and performance, our staff ensures every player receives the highest level of training and care.

Head Coach

John Frank

John Frank has been the driving force behind our club’s success for over a decade. With more than 15 years of coaching experience, he has guided teams to multiple regional championships and earned recognition for developing young talent into elite athletes. Known for his tactical intelligence and strong leadership, Coach Frank has won Coach of the Year twice and continues to set the standard for excellence at the club.

Assistant Coaches

James Herdson

James brings over 10 years of experience in youth and senior football development. He specializes in offensive strategy and player conditioning. During his time with the club, he has helped secure two league titles and played a major role in elevating the performance of our midfielders and forwards.

Clark Jones

Clark is a former semi-professional player turned coach, known for his defensive expertise. With 8 years of coaching experience, he focuses on strengthening team structure, discipline, and on-field communication. His defensive units have consistently ranked among the top in the league under his guidance.

Maria Sett

Maria has a strong background in technical training and youth development. She has worked with academies across the Caribbean and brings 6 years of experience shaping young players into confident, well-rounded athletes. Maria played an important role in the club’s U17 championship victory, focusing on skill development and mental preparation.

Goalkeeper Coach

Joe Clap

Joe is a former professional goalkeeper with over 12 years of experience in specialized goalkeeper training. He has coached multiple keepers who went on to play at national youth levels. Joe emphasizes reflex training, footwork, and game awareness, helping our keepers achieve top save percentages season after season.

Medical Staff

Sarah Lewis

Sarah Lewis is a certified sports therapist with a background in injury prevention, rehabilitation, and conditioning. With 7+ years of field experience, she has supported athletes at regional tournaments and ensures our players stay healthy and match-ready. Her expertise has significantly reduced injury downtime within the club.
